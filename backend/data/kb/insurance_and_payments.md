# Insurance and Payments

## Accepted insurance providers

Mykare Health has cashless tie-ups with major Indian health insurers including:

- Star Health
- HDFC ERGO Health
- ICICI Lombard
- Niva Bupa (formerly Max Bupa)
- New India Assurance
- Aditya Birla Health
- Tata AIG

For other insurers, we provide all necessary bills and documentation for reimbursement claims.

## What insurance covers

- OPD consultation fees (subject to your policy's OPD benefit)
- Diagnostic tests done on-site (if covered by your plan)
- Pharmacy purchases at our in-house pharmacy

Cosmetic procedures and elective wellness checks are generally not covered — please confirm with your provider before the visit.

## Payment modes

We accept cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), all major credit and debit cards, and net banking. GST-compliant invoices are issued at the front desk.

## Pre-authorisation

For planned procedures requiring cashless authorisation, please contact our billing desk at least 48 hours in advance with your policy number and ID proof. Approval typically takes 24 hours.

## Estimates

For any consultation or test, you can request a written cost estimate before proceeding. Final charges may vary slightly based on additional tests or services rendered.

## Refunds

Refunds for cancelled appointments are processed back to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
