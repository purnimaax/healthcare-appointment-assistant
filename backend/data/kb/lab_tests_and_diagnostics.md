# Lab Tests and Diagnostics

## In-house tests

Mykare Health has an on-site lab offering:
- Complete Blood Count (CBC)
- Lipid Profile
- Blood Glucose (Fasting, Post-Prandial, HbA1c)
- Liver Function Test (LFT)
- Kidney Function Test (KFT)
- Thyroid Profile (TSH, T3, T4)
- Urine Routine Examination
- ECG and Echocardiogram (Cardiology dept)
- X-ray (Orthopedics dept)

## Test turnaround time

| Test category | Typical TAT |
| --- | --- |
| Routine blood tests | Same day (4–6 hours) |
| Thyroid, hormone panels | Next day |
| Special pathology / culture | 2–3 days |
| Imaging (X-ray) | Same day |
| ECG / Echo report | Within 24 hours |

## Sample collection

Walk-in sample collection runs from 7:30 AM to 11:00 AM, Monday through Saturday. Home collection is available for an additional fee — call the front desk to book.

## Fasting requirements

- **Fasting blood sugar, lipid profile**: 8–10 hours fasting, water is allowed.
- **HbA1c, thyroid, CBC**: No fasting required.
- **Liver function test**: 8 hours fasting preferred (not strictly required).

## Getting your reports

Reports are sent to the email on file as soon as they are released. You can also pick them up at the front desk with a photo ID. The doctor's interpretation is usually scheduled as a follow-up consultation — this can be booked through the assistant.

## Imaging interpretation

X-rays, ECGs, and echocardiograms are reviewed by the consulting specialist during your appointment. We do not provide standalone radiology reports without a consultation, as interpretation depends on clinical context.
