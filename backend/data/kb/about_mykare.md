# About Mykare Health

Mykare Health is a multi-speciality clinic offering outpatient consultations across six departments. We operate Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

## Departments and what they treat

**General Medicine** — first point of contact for fevers, infections, fatigue, chronic conditions (diabetes, hypertension), preventive health checks, and general wellness queries. If you are not sure which speciality you need, start here.

**Cardiology** — heart-related concerns including chest pain, palpitations, high blood pressure follow-up, ECG / echo interpretation, post-cardiac event follow-up, and lipid disorders.

**Orthopedics** — bones, joints, and musculoskeletal issues. Common reasons to visit: back pain, knee or shoulder pain, sports injuries, fracture follow-up, and arthritis management.

**Dermatology** — skin, hair, and nail conditions. Includes acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, hair loss, allergic skin reactions, and cosmetic concerns like pigmentation.

**Pediatrics** — care for children aged 0–16 years. Routine vaccinations, growth and developmental checks, fever and infections in children, allergies, and adolescent health.

**ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)** — ear pain, hearing problems, recurrent throat infections, sinusitis, allergies affecting the airway, snoring, and voice problems.
