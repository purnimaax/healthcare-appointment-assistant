# Appointment Policies

## Booking window

Appointments can be booked up to 14 days in advance. Same-day bookings are accepted subject to slot availability.

## Slot duration

Each consultation slot is 30 minutes. Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled time to complete any required paperwork.

## Cancellation policy

- Cancellation more than 4 hours before the appointment: no charge.
- Cancellation within 4 hours: a small no-show fee may apply at the doctor's discretion.
- To cancel, simply ask the assistant or use the Appointments panel — you'll need your registered phone number or email.

## Rescheduling

You can reschedule your appointment any number of times up to 1 hour before the slot. Provide your appointment ID (received at booking) or look it up by your phone number.

## What to bring

- Government-issued photo ID
- Previous prescriptions or reports relevant to your visit
- List of current medications (including over-the-counter and supplements)
- For follow-ups: the original consultation summary

## Late arrivals

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, the doctor may need to see other patients first. We will accommodate you in the next available gap, but cannot guarantee your original slot.

## Companions and minors

Patients under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian. Adult patients may bring one companion into the consultation room.
